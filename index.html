About me

Let me tell you about my personal life. Growing up in Spain, even though I'm Romanian by birth, has given me a unique perspective on the world. I've always been fascinated by different cultures, which is why I'm currently learning Japanese. Soon, I'll be able to speak four languages fluently, and I couldn't be more excited about it! Back when I was in 6th form not too long ago, my favorite subject was IT. Specifically, I loved diving into the cyber security module during my second year. Learning about encryption, data management, and the importance of securing data really sparked my interest. It's amazing how crucial these skills are in today's digital world, and I'm eager to keep exploring and learning more about them.

So, currently these are the languages that I am able to speak:

Romanian

Romania will always hold a special place in my heart as my first home. Its unforgettable cuisine, stunning yet straightforward landscapes, and rich culture never fail to fill me with nostalgia and joy. And let's not forget the winters there - they're the best, with snow blanketing everything!

English

I didn't move to England until I was 15 years old. Initially, it felt quite daunting to me, yet incredibly thrilling at the same time, knowing I would encounter people from various nations and make countless friends. And indeed, I was proven correct! Since arriving here, not only has my English improved significantly, but my confidence in meeting new people has soared!

Spanish

Having resided here for 12 years, I must say, I have developed a deep admiration for Spanish culture. The Latin music, cuisine, climate, and overall charm have left an indelible mark on my life. The language itself, with its beauty and romanticism, continues to captivate me endlessly. Summers here are just perfect. Mixing the hot Spanish sunsets with some bachata music soothes the soul like nothing else!

Japanese

What captivates me about Japanese culture is their boundless creativity and deep passion for art and tradition. As an artist, I've found immense inspiration in anime culture, which has fueled my own creativity and passion for art significantly. Additionally, I admire the Japanese language and the genuine kindness shown by the people - it's simply wonderful.
