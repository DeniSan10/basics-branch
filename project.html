Denis' Mini Projects!

In this section you will be able to see mini projects that I have done!

I'll be showcasing four sections in total, with the first three being art related: the first sections will feature projects where I was involved in, one currently ongoing and another from the past. The other section will showcase artistic activities I do in my free time, so you can just call them mini projects!

The last project will be a placeholder where my future javascript project will take place!

Project number 1:

Lunarium

I'm working on a novel about an ancient legend from Egypt. Long ago, a group of pharaohs found a powerful moonstone. Its discovery brought chaos to our world for centuries, and its curse still lingers today, haunting us through the ages. Will the legendary hero mentioned in the ancient scriptures of thoth change this cruel fate?

Here are some digital illustrations I've created for the novel. Initially, I planned to make a 2D shooting game, but I realized I was missing a crucial element: a main plot! So, I shifted gears to writing a novel. This way, I can craft a rich story that could be adapted into a game, movie, or anime. For now though, I hope these illustrations give you an idea of my intentions are!

Project number 2:

Captivity

During my Sixth Form studies, I worked on an art and design project focused on Captivity. I chose this topic to raise awareness about how animals are treated by us, humans. The project lasted for two years and, surprisingly, involved a lot of writing as well! These are some of my work examples including the exposition!

As you can see, there is also a painting of myself standing on a platform around some beautiful clouds. This was an extended course exam on where we had to choose an additional theme for another time-constrained coursework, and I had chosen portraiture. I decided to go with something calm this time to also have the ability to express my open-mindedness and passion for life.
