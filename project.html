Denis' Mini Projects!

In this section you will be able to see mini projects that I have done!

I'll be showcasing four sections in forms of sliders in total: two mini projects which I do in my free time, and two other big projects where I was involved in, one currently ongoing and another from the past, please enjoy!.

Project number 1:

Lunarium

I'm working on a novel about an ancient legend from Egypt. Long ago, a group of pharaohs found a powerful moonstone. Its discovery brought chaos to our world for centuries, and its curse still lingers today, haunting us through the ages. Will the legendary hero mentioned in the ancient scriptures of thoth change this cruel fate?

Here are some digital illustrations I've created for the novel. Initially, I planned to make a 2D shooting game, but I realized I was missing a crucial element: a main plot! So, I shifted gears to writing a novel. This way, I can craft a rich story that could be adapted into a game, movie, or anime. For now though, I hope these illustrations give you an idea of my intentions are!
